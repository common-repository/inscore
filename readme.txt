== Description ==

WP inScore plugin allows you to integrate free sport live scores provided by Livescore.in into your wordpress website.

Do you have a sports related website? You can enhance your website content by implementing live scores from all major leagues for free! Configure your own livescore service at http://www.livescore.in/free-livescore/ and start using the inScore solution within just 5 minutes.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= What is inScore? =

The inScore solution offers free sport live scores for your website.

= How can I get my inScore? =

It is very easy, just register at http://www.livescore.in/free-livescore/. Configuration will take less than 5 minutes.

= Where can I find my inScore ID? =

Log in to your account at http://www.livescore.in/free-livescore/ and check your HTML/PHP code. You can find your inScore ID under the code.

= I have a problem with the advanced PHP version of inScore. Can you help me? =

Read our FAQ here: http://www.livescore.in/free-livescore/faq-php/.
